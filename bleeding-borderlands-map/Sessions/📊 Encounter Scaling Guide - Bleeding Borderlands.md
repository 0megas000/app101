## Encounter Building Quick Reference

**Easy Encounter:** 25% of daily XP budget (travel, optional fights)  
**Medium Encounter:** 50% of daily XP budget (standard challenge)  
**Hard Encounter:** 75% of daily XP budget (major fight)  
**Deadly Encounter:** 100%+ of daily XP budget (boss fight, should be rare)

### üåÄ Corruption-Touched (Level 1-2 Corruption)

**Low Level (CR 1/2 - 2):**
- **Phase Wolf** (CR 1) - Wolf that can briefly turn incorporeal
  - *Reskin: Wolf with Misty Step 1/day*
- **Reality Flicker Bat** (CR 1/4) - Swarm that phases
  - *Reskin: Swarm of Bats with advantage on first attack (appears from nowhere)*
- **Echo Beast** (CR 1) - Translucent predator from another plane
  - *Reskin: Shadow with force damage instead of necrotic*

**Mid Level (CR 3-5):**
- **Dimensional Bear** (CR 3) - Partially phased, attacks ignore some armor
  - *Reskin: Brown Bear, attacks count as magical and ignore +1 AC from armor*
- **Phase Cat** (CR 2) - Medium cat, teleports short distances
  - *Reskin: Panther with Misty Step 3/day, 15-foot range*
- **Corruption Hound** (CR 2) - Tracks by sensing dimensional instability
  - *Reskin: Hell Hound, fire damage becomes force damage, tracks corrupted creatures*

**Usage:** Wilderness travel, corruption zone encounters, early horror

## Encounter Modifiers

### Environmental Factors

**Level 1 Corruption (+0 CR):**
- Occasional reality flickers (Perception disadvantage)
- Minor gravity shifts (Dex save DC 10 or prone 1/combat)
- Wild magic on natural 1s

**Level 2 Corruption (+1 CR):**
- Random teleportation (1d6√ó10 feet per hour)
- Equipment phases out (25% chance per encounter)
- Time distortion (initiative rerolls mid-combat)

**Level 3 Corruption (+2 CR):**
- Physical transformation risks (Con saves or corruption points)
- Reality attacks (3d10 force damage randomly)
- Planar overlaps (additional creatures appear)

**Level 4 Corruption (+3 CR):**
- Should be near-impossible without [[The Pilgrims|Pilgrim]] guide
- Reality actively hostile
- Survival itself the challenge

### Faction Modifiers

**Vigilant Order:** Add divine damage, healing support, organized tactics  
**Ascendant Cult:** Add corruption abilities, unpredictable behavior, transformation mid-combat  
**Empirical Academy:** Add prepared defenses, clever tactics, magical items  
**Pragmatic Coalition:** Add reinforcements, practical weapons, coordinated attacks  
**The Pilgrims:** Add phase abilities, corruption immunity, reality navigation

## Creating Custom Corruption Creatures

**Template: Any Base Creature ‚Üí Corruption-Touched**

1. Keep base stats
2. Add **Phase Step** (bonus action, teleport 15 feet, 1/day)
3. Add **Corruption Resistance** (advantage on saves vs. corruption)
4. Change damage type to Force or Psychic
5. Add **Reality Flicker** (disadvantage on opportunity attacks against it)

**Increase CR by +1**

**Example:**
- Base: Wolf (CR 1/4)
- Add above abilities
- Result: Phase Wolf (CR 1)

## Boss Fight Design Tips

**Multi-Phase Fights:**
- Phase 1: Normal combat
- Phase 2 (50% HP): Environmental change or reinforcements
- Phase 3 (25% HP): Desperate action or transformation

**Lair Actions:** Major NPCs in their bases get +1 action per round (initiative 20)

**Legendary Actions:** Major NPCs get 3/round, can interrupt players

**Minions:** Boss + 1/4 CR minions (4 per player) that die in 1 hit make fights dynamic

**Escape Clause:** Most intelligent enemies flee at 25% HP unless fanatic
