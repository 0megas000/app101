## **Core Philosophy**
The sleeping gods are false demons masquerading as divinity. True divine authority comes through traditional religious hierarchy and blessed wealth. Only proper faith, backed by military might and economic power, can restore righteous order to a world corrupted by heretical thinking.

## **Goals & Strategy**
### **Immediate (1-3 months):**
- Consolidate military and financial resources for major campaign
- Suppress heretical factions through economic and social pressure
- Establish blessed barriers around controlled territories
- Recruit faithful from refugee populations

### **Medium-term (3-12 months):**
- Launch coordinated crusade against corruption sources
- Establish theocratic governance over liberated territories
- Eliminate or convert heretical faction leadership
- Create holy empire spanning the entire region

### **Ultimate Goal:**
Total restoration of divine order through a new holy empire, with the Vigilant Order as supreme religious and temporal authority, all traces of false gods destroyed, and proper faith governing all aspects of life.

## **Typical Member Profile**
### **Elite Templars:**
Professional holy warriors combining military skill with religious devotion. Often from noble or wealthy backgrounds, seeking to serve divine will through strength of arms. Absolutely loyal to Order hierarchy.

### **Faithful Refugees:**
Displaced people who find comfort in Order's combination of spiritual guidance and material security. Grateful for protection and aid, willing to support religious authority that provides stability.

### **Wealthy Converts:**
Merchants, nobles, and professionals who align with Order for economic and social advantages. May be genuinely faithful or simply pragmatic about supporting the strongest faction.

### **Traditional Believers:**
Long-term faithful who see the Order as legitimate religious authority. Often older individuals who remember pre-crisis religious structures and want restoration of traditional values.
