## **Core Philosophy**
The sleeping gods are merely powerful extraplanar entities that can be understood, measured, and controlled through scientific methodology. Technology and empirical knowledge will solve the corruption crisis without requiring dangerous divine intervention.

## **Goals & Strategy**
### **Immediate (1-3 months):**
- Establish field research stations around corruption zones
- Develop technological detection and measurement tools
- Recruit displaced scholars and fund anti-religious research
- Prove corruption has scientific rather than divine causes

### **Medium-term (3-12 months):**
- Create technological barriers to contain corruption spread
- Develop methods to reverse reality distortion through science
- Establish Academy as premier authority on planar phenomena
- Discredit religious explanations through public education

### **Ultimate Goal:**
Complete scientific mastery of planar convergence, proving divine intervention unnecessary while establishing rational, academic governance of crisis response.

## **Typical Member Profile**
### **Faculty & Researchers:**
Displaced academics, former university professors, brilliant students who survived the crisis. Motivated by intellectual curiosity and belief in rational solutions. Often traumatized by loss of previous academic institutions.

### **Student Volunteers:**
Young people seeking education and purpose amid crisis. Mix of pre-corruption students and refugee youth. Range from genuinely committed to science to simply seeking safety and meals.

### **Support Staff:**
Craftsmen, guards, and administrators who value the Academy's organized approach and educational opportunities. Often families of academics or individuals impressed by technological solutions.
