**Motto:** "Solutions, not prayers"

## **Leadership**
- **Primary:** [[Chancellor Mira Ashford]] - Former university administrator turned crisis manager
- **Military:** [[Captain Marcus Steelwright]] - Ex-military engineer leading refugee operations
- **Economic:** [[Merchant-Lord Cassius Vale]] - Wealthy trader funding Coalition operations
- **Field:** Multiple regional coordinators managing local operations

## **Core Philosophy**
Divine and theoretical solutions waste time while people suffer and die. Practical organization, efficient resource distribution, and human compassion can maintain civilization until the crisis resolves. Focus on saving lives and preserving society, not debating causes or seeking perfect solutions.

## **Goals & Strategy**
### **Immediate (Ongoing):**
- Process and house refugee populations safely and humanely
- Maintain supply lines and distribution networks
- Coordinate between settlements for mutual aid
- Document crisis progression for future planning

### **Medium-term (6-18 months):**
- Establish permanent settlements for displaced populations
- Develop sustainable resource production and distribution
- Create defensive infrastructure protecting population centers
- Build political framework for post-crisis governance

### **Ultimate Goal:**
Preserve human civilization and society through the crisis period, ensuring that whatever emerges afterward maintains humanitarian values and practical governance rather than extremist ideology.
