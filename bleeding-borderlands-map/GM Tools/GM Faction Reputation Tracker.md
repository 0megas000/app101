# GM Faction Reputation Tracker

## Quick Reference Scale

- **+5 or higher:** Devoted Ally (will take significant risks for party)
- **+3 to +4:** Trusted (preferential treatment, shares information)
- **+1 to +2:** Friendly (basic cooperation, fair deals)
- **0:** Neutral (purely transactional)
- **-1 to -2:** Suspicious (limited interaction, higher prices)
- **-3 to -4:** Hostile (active obstruction, may refuse service)
- **-5 or lower:** Enemy (will attack on sight or actively hunt)

## Quick Event Consequences

### High Reputation Scene (+3 or more)

- Faction seeks party out for important missions
- Provides aid without being asked
- Shares secret information
- Offers membership/positions
- Defends party to others

### Neutral Reputation Scene (0)

- Business as usual
- Fair prices, standard services
- No special treatment either way
- Information for payment only
- Won't take risks for party

### Low Reputation Scene (-3 or worse)

- Refuses services/aid
- Spreads negative rumors
- Actively works against party
- May attack if opportunity arises
- Hires others to harass party

## Campaign Reputation Events

**Overall Reputation Thresholds:**

- Total +10: Party seen as heroes by most
- Total +5: Generally respected
- Total 0: Unknown quantities
- Total -5: Troublemakers
- Total -10: Enemies of civilization

**Faction War Triggers:**

- Any two factions at opposite extremes (+5/-5) = conflict
- Party must choose sides or become casualties
- Neutral factions pulled into conflict
- City-wide consequences
