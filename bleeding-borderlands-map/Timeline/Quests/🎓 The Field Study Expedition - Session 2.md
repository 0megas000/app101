## The Crisis Point - Who to Save?

### The Situation Explodes

**Multiple Crises Simultaneously:**

1. **Sick Child** - Needs medicine NOW or dies
2. **Pilgrim Conversion** - 3 families about to undergo transformation
3. **Faction Conflict** - Vigilant/Academy agents about to fight
4. **Corruption Surge** - Zone expanding rapidly
5. **Missing People** - 5 villagers lost in deep corruption

**Party Must Choose:** Can only resolve 2 before escape

### Resolution Options

**Save the Child:**

- Use Coalition medicine (if have it)
- Stabilizes situation temporarily
- Families grateful, trust party
- Coalition rep +2

**Stop Pilgrim Conversion:**

- Persuasion DC 16 or Combat
- Save 3 families from transformation
- Pilgrims become hostile
- Major faction reputation shifts

**Prevent Faction Fight:**

- Diplomacy DC 18 or pick side
- Reveal larger threat to unite them
- Changes campaign dynamics
- Gain unexpected allies

**Outrun Corruption:**

- Athletics/Survival checks
- Save everyone but gain corruption
- Dramatic escape sequence
- Equipment damaged/lost

**Rescue Missing:**

- Venture deeper into corruption
- Find them transformed/dead/transcended
- Learn crucial campaign secret
- Highest risk/reward

### The Escape

**Regardless of Choices:**

_The corruption surge hits like a tide of impossible color_

**Escape Sequence:**

- Ground becomes uncertain
- Distance stops making sense
- Reality tears show other worlds
- Something massive stirs beneath

**Skill Challenge:** 3 successes before 3 failures

- Athletics: Run on shifting ground
- Acrobatics: Dodge phasing debris
- Perception: Spot safe paths
- Survival: Navigate chaos
- Arcana: Predict reality shifts

**Success:** Escape with samples and survivors **Failure:** Lost in corruption, strange rescue
