## Quick Resolution Tables

### Faction Reputation Final Tally:

- **+4 or more:** Trusted ally, special privileges
- **+1 to +3:** Favorable, basic cooperation
- **0:** Neutral, purely transactional
- **-1 to -3:** Suspicious, limited interaction
- **-4 or less:** Enemy, active opposition

### Corruption Points Resolution:

- **0-3:** No lasting effects
- **4-6:** Minor physical mark, occasional visions
- **7-9:** Notable transformation, NPC reactions
- **10-12:** Major changes, mechanical effects
- **13+:** Approaching NPC status, final warning
