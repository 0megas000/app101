## Description

Sprawling tent city and makeshift housing extending beyond [[ğŸ“ Thornwick Academy]]'s original walls. What began as temporary refugee overflow two years ago has become semi-permanent settlement. Mix of canvas tents, wooden shacks, and repurposed wagons housing displaced people from corrupted regions.

**Layout:**
- No planned organization, organic growth
- Narrow dirt paths between shelters
- Central areas more established, edges newest arrivals
- Some areas have names (Camp Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Delta)
- Outer edge constantly expanding as more refugees arrive

**Conditions:**
- Overcrowded (designed for 1,000, holds 6,500)
- Poor sanitation (open sewage, disease risk)
- Limited clean water (rationed from city wells)
- Food shortages (aid distribution never enough)
- Crime problems (desperation breeds theft)
- Fire hazards (canvas and wood, cooking fires)

## Daily Life

**Dawn (6am):**
- Wake to cold, damp conditions
- Breakfast lines at aid stations
- Job seekers head toward city

**Morning (6am-12pm):**
- Those with work leave camps
- Children attend informal schools
- Volunteers run aid stations
- Elderly tend small gardens

**Midday (12pm-6pm):**
- Lunch distribution (never enough)
- Heat in summer (tents like ovens)
- Mud in rain (sanitation nightmare)
- Workers return from city

**Evening (6pm-11pm):**
- Dinner lines (largest meal)
- Community gatherings around fires
- Stories told, songs sung
- Hope shared and tears shed

**Night (11pm-6am):**
- Cold, fear, uncertainty
- Guards patrol (minimal)
- Crying children
- Prayers whispered
