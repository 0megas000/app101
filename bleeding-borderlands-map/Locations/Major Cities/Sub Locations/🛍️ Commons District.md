## Daily Life

**Morning (6am-12pm):**
- Market vendors set up
- Students rush to morning classes
- Workers open shops
- Breakfast crowds at taverns

**Afternoon (12pm-6pm):**
- Peak shopping hours
- Students between classes
- Street performers appear
- Food vendors everywhere

**Evening (6pm-11pm):**
- Taverns fill up
- Students socialize
- Shops close (except taverns)
- Street lamps lit by lamplighters

**Night (11pm-6am):**
- Taverns close at 2am
- City Watch patrols increase
- Occasional drunk student incidents
- Quiet streets
