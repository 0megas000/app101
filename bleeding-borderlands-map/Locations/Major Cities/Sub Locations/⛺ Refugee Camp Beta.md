# Refugee Camp Beta

**Type:** Refugee Settlement | **Location:** Thornwick Outskirts (3 miles east)  
**Population:** 1,400 | **Faction:** Empirical Academy (research focus)  
**Conditions:** Better organized, experimental | **Security:** Moderate

## Overview

Academy runs this camp as testbed for "scientific refugee management." Better organized than others, but refugees are study subjects. Medical experiments (with "consent"), corruption resistance testing, psychological studies. Ethical lines blur.

## Features

- Research tents (medical experiments, DC 18 Medicine to understand)
- Better supplies (Academy funded)
- Education programs (teach refugees useful skills)
- Observation (refugees know they're being watched)

## D&D 5e Mechanics

**Living Here:** Free (Academy pays), but must participate in studies  
**Escape:** DC 14 Stealth (guards watch for test subjects leaving)  
**Evidence:** DC 16 Investigation to find unethical experiment records

## Plot Hooks

- Expose unethical experiments
- Rescue unwilling test subject
- Steal research data
- Academy offers party payment for "volunteering"
