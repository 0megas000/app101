## **Overview**
An ancient military fortress consumed by the Maelstrom's corruption and claimed by the Ascendant Cult as their sacred home. The stone walls now shift and breathe like living tissue, doors open onto different rooms each time, and hallways stretch or compress based on incomprehensible patterns. To outsiders, it's a nightmare maze. To the cultists, it's paradise‚Äîthey walk through the chaos with complete confidence, reading the corruption like others read signposts.

The Commune serves as both religious center and transformation sanctuary, where the faithful gather to accelerate their change into something "more than human."

## **Physical Appearance**

### **Exterior:**
- **Original Structure:** Square stone fortress with four corner towers
- **Corrupted Reality:** Walls phase between solid stone and translucent energy
- **Color Shifts:** Stone cycles through natural gray to impossible purples and blacks
- **Gravity Defiance:** Parts of walls grow sideways or upside-down
- **Living Architecture:** Cracks seal themselves, windows appear and vanish
- **The Threshold:** Main gate pulses like a heartbeat, sometimes three gates, sometimes none

### **Interior:**
- **Shifting Layout:** Rooms rearrange constantly‚Äîonly cultists navigate reliably
- **Organic Growth:** Stone floors sprout crystal formations overnight
- **Dimensional Overlap:** See into adjacent rooms through reality tears
- **Impossible Geometry:** Staircases lead both up and down simultaneously
- **Breathing Walls:** Structure inhales/exhales, expanding and contracting
- **Light Sources:** Corruption energy provides eerie purple-white illumination

## **Security & Defenses**

### **Entry Control:**
- **Outsiders Unwelcome:** Assumption of hostility toward non-members
- **Evaluation Process:** Those seeking transformation are tested for sincerity
- **Corruption Test:** Newcomers must survive exposure in controlled environment
- **Failed Entry:** Those rejected are driven off or killed if they resist
- **Secret Paths:** Only cultists know stable routes through Middle Ring to Commune

### **Defensive Measures:**
- **Transformed Guards:** Enhanced by corruption, dangerous combatants
- **Reality Traps:** Shifting architecture confuses and disorients intruders
- **Corruption Weapons:** Weapons that cause transformation on contact
- **Emergency Protocols:** Can seal entire sections or collapse passages
- **Malachar's Favor:** Believe divine protection shields them from harm

### **External Activities:**
- **Recruitment Cells:** Operating in [[üéì Thornwick Academy]], [[‚öúÔ∏è Goldspire]], other cities

## **Daily Life in the Commune**

### **Routine:**
- Wake at dawn for prayer facing Maelstrom
- Communal breakfast (corrupted food that tastes normal to them)
- Morning work: maintenance, training, ritual preparation
- Midday transformation progress check-ins
- Afternoon: personal meditation or assigned duties
- Evening: mass gathering in Worship Hall
- Night: individual transformation acceleration sessions

### **Culture:**
- **No Personal Property:** Everything shared among believers
- **Transformation Celebrated:** Each new change is cause for joy
- **Hierarchy by Change:** More transformed = more respected
- **Aggressive Defense:** Any threat met with overwhelming force
- **Malachar-Centric:** All conversations eventually return to their god
- **Radical Acceptance:** Former identities abandoned, only "what we become" matters

### **Sustenance:**
- **Corrupted Food:** Supplies transformed by Maelstrom energy
- **Safe for Believers:** Their changed bodies process it normally
- **Deadly for Outsiders:** Would cause severe transformation/illness
- **Water from Below:** Underground springs corrupted but clean
- **Lysander's Supplies:** Some normal food for newest members

## **DM Notes**

### **Running the Commune:**
- Emphasize the wrongness‚Äîeverything is slightly off, unsettling
- Cultists should seem both pitiful and terrifying
- Use shifting architecture to create tension and confusion
- Make transformation seem both beautiful and horrifying to them

### **Hostile Encounters:**
- Cultists fight fanatically, no fear of death
- They use corruption as weapon‚Äîinfecting enemies
- Retreat is rare‚Äîthey defend their home to the end
- Captured enemies are either converted or killed

### **Moral Complexity:**
- Many members were desperate refugees seeking acceptance
- Some genuinely find peace in transformation
- Not all are evil‚Äîjust radically committed to different future
- Players might sympathize with some individual cultists

### **Balance:**
- Don't make them cartoon villains
- Show their humanity even as they abandon it
- Give them legitimate grievances against other factions
- Make players question if they're entirely wrong
