# The Planning Center

**Type:** Strategic Command | **Location:** Haven's Rest (Administrative Complex, 3rd Floor)  
**Size:** Large room (2,000 sq ft) | **Access:** Restricted (officers only)  
**Faction:** Pragmatic Coalition | **Activity:** 24/7 operations

## Overview

War room for refugee crisis management. Walls covered in maps marked with corruption spread, refugee movements, supply lines, and faction positions. Three large tables with terrain models. Officers huddle over reports, updating positions hourly.

## Key Features

**Central Map Table:**

- 20ft x 15ft regional map with movable markers
- Corruption zones marked in red (updated daily)
- Refugee camps (blue flags)
- Supply routes (green lines)
- Faction positions (colored pins)

**Intelligence Wall:**

- Daily reports from field agents
- Corruption spread measurements
- Resource availability charts
- Weather forecasts
- Faction movement tracking

**Communication Station:**

- Sending Stones to major outposts (8 total)
- Message sorting desk
- Cipher books (DC 20 to decode enemy messages)
- Carrier pigeon coops

**Planning Tables:**

- Evacuation simulations
- Supply distribution optimization
- Construction project timelines
- Military deployment options

## D&D 5e Mechanics

**Access:** DC 20 Deception (pass as officer), DC 22 Stealth (sneak in)  
**Documents:** Intelligence reports worth 100-1,000 GP to enemies  
**Staff Present:** 8-12 officers (CR 3-5) at all times  
**Security:** 4 guards at door (CR 2)

**Information Available:**

- Faction movements (exact positions)
- Corruption predictions (3-day forecast)
- Supply stockpiles (quantities, locations)
- Secret operations (Coalition black ops)
- Weaknesses in defenses

## NPCs

**Captain Marcus Steelwright** (CR 7, chief strategist)  
**12 Planning Officers** (CR 3-5)  
**Chancellor Ashford** (CR 5, frequent visitor)

## Plot Hooks

- Steal military intelligence
- Plant false information
- Assassinate planning staff
- Copy maps for rival faction
- Warn Coalition of incoming threat
