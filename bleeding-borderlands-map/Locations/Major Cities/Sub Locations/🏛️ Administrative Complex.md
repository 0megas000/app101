# Administrative Complex

**Type:** Government Building | **Location:** Haven's Rest (Central District)  
**Faction:** Pragmatic Coalition (Headquarters) | **Size:** Large (4 floors)  
**Population:** 200 staff | **Security:** Moderate (DC 18 to infiltrate)

## Overview

The nerve center of refugee crisis management. This fortified brick building houses the Pragmatic Coalition's leadership and coordinates all regional relief operations. Maps cover every wall, messengers rush through halls, and exhausted administrators work around the clock managing the greatest humanitarian crisis in history.

## Key Features

**Ground Floor:**

- Reception hall (visitors processed)
- Message center (ravens, runners, sending stones)
- Map room (regional situation updates hourly)
- Guard station (12 guards, CR 2)

**Second Floor:**

- Chancellor Mira Ashford's office (corner suite, well-guarded)
- Council chamber (faction meetings)
- Records archive (refugees, supplies, operations)
- Clerk offices (20 administrators)

**Third Floor:**

- Strategic planning rooms (war room setup)
- Intelligence analysis (faction movements tracked)
- Communication hub (magical/mundane)
- Staff quarters (night shift accommodation)

**Fourth Floor/Roof:**

- Signal tower (flags, lights, heliograph)
- Observation post (watch for threats)
- Emergency evacuation point (rope ladders)
- Storage (emergency supplies)

## NPCs

**Chancellor Mira Ashford** (CR 5, Leader)

## D&D 5e Mechanics

**Infiltration:** DC 18 Stealth (guards patrol every 15 minutes)  
**Social Entry:** DC 15 Persuasion with appointment, DC 20 without  
**Key Documents:** Intelligence reports, supply manifests, faction correspondence

**Security:**

- Guards check credentials (DC 15 Deception to fake)
- Sending Stones alert to Golden Citadel, Thornwick
- Alarm spell on Chancellor's office (DC 20 Investigation to find)
- Safe in office (DC 22 Thieves' Tools to open, 2,000 GP emergency funds)

## Plot Hooks

- Steal/plant documents
- Assassination attempt on Ashford
- Faction spy infiltration
- Emergency crisis response
- Negotiate with Coalition leadership
