# Communication Hub

**Type:** Communications Facility | **Location:** Haven's Rest (Administrative Complex, 3rd Floor)  
**Size:** Medium room | **Staff:** 12 operators | **Activity:** 24/7

## Overview

Regional message coordination center. Sending stones, carrier pigeons, signal flags, and messenger runners all converge here. Every communication into or out of Haven's Rest passes through this room.

## Key Features

**Sending Stone Network:** 8 paired stones connecting to major locations (1,000 GP each, 100-mile range)  
**Pigeon Coop:** 200 trained ravens/pigeons (messages 50 miles in 2 hours)  
**Signal Tower Access:** Heliograph and flag signals (line of sight, 10 miles)  
**Cipher Desk:** Encode/decode secret messages (DC 18 Intelligence to crack)

## D&D 5e Mechanics

**Intercept Message:** DC 20 Stealth + DC 18 Sleight of Hand  
**Send False Message:** DC 18 Deception (must have code phrases)  
**Tap Sending Stone:** DC 22 Arcana (permanent link, eavesdrop)

## NPCs

**Chief Operator Maris** (CR 3, knows all codes)  
**12 Message Operators** (CR 1)
