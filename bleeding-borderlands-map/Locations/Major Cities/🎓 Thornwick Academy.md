## **Overview**
Rising from rolling hills like a beacon of civilization and learning, Thornwick Academy represents the triumph of knowledge over chaos. The city spreads organically across seven hills, with the crystalline dome of [[ğŸ—¼ Thornwick's Spire]] serving as both literal lighthouse and metaphorical symbol of hope. From fifteen miles away, travelers can see the spire's ethereal blue-white glow, a reassuring constant in an increasingly chaotic world.

The air carries the distinctive scent of old parchment, alchemical reagents, and that metallic tang that precedes a thunderstorm. Ancient oak trees line cobblestone streets worn smooth by centuries of student feet, their leaves whispering with an almost intelligent rustle. Architecture blends centuries of styles, from original stone foundations to hasty wooden refugee structures, creating a living timeline of crisis and adaptation.

## **Major Landmarks**

### **[[ğŸ—¼ Thornwick's Spire]]** 
The 180-foot tower dominating the skyline, crowned with a crystalline dome that serves as both observatory and beacon. During the day, the crystal focuses sunlight into brilliant displays visible for miles. At night, it glows with soft blue radiance that provides hope and navigation aid to travelers. The spire houses the university's most advanced astronomical equipment and serves as a symbol of academic achievement.

### **[[âš°ï¸ Memorial Gardens]]**
Solemn space containing 300 graves of corruption victims, each marked with personalized stones bearing names and carved symbols representing their lives. The garden is maintained by volunteers and filled with flowers that bloom in impossible colors - a beautiful reminder of lives lost to dimensional instability.

### **[[ğŸ”’ Quarantine Vaults]]**
Underground complex carved from solid stone where corrupted artifacts are stored and studied. The vaults feature multiple containment systems including lead lining, magical wards, and dimensional anchor points to prevent artifacts from phasing between realities.

### **[[ğŸŒ‰ Scholar's Bridge]]**
Once-proud stone arch spanning Brightwater Stream, now serving as visible reminder of corruption's advance. The eastern side exists in normal reality while the western side shimmers between dimensions, creating a bridge that leads to somewhere else entirely. Students dare each other to cross it, though university officials forbid the practice.
