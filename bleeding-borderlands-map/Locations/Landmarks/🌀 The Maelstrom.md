# The Maelstrom

**Type:** Corruption Zone - Planar Convergence | **Diameter:** 32 miles (circular with spreading arms) **Center Point:** Former trade intersection of three kingdoms **Threat Level:** Extreme - Reality-warping corruption storm **Related God:** [[ğŸŒ€ Malachar the Transformer]]

## **Overview**

A massive dimensional storm where reality bleeds between planes, appearing as a churning mass of purple-black clouds with impossible physics. Lightning crackles sideways, rain falls upward, and the very fabric of existence tears and reforms moment by moment. From a distance, it resembles a hurricane of cosmic proportions, but instead of wind and water, it's made of raw transformation energy.

The Maelstrom pulses like a living thing, expanding slowly but inexorably at 200-300 feet per month, with devastating surges reaching 2 miles per day when conditions align.

## **Physical Appearance**

- **Storm Clouds:** Deep purple with black veins of anti-light
- **Energy Patterns:** Crackling prismatic lightning moving in impossible directions
- **Gravity Anomalies:** Objects floating, spinning, or compressed at random
- **Sound:** A constant low hum punctuated by reality-tearing cracks
- **Smell:** Ozone mixed with something indescribable - the scent of "change itself"
- **Shape:** Mostly circular with irregular arms/pockets extending outward where corruption spreads faster

## **The Surge Phenomenon**

Approximately once per month, the Maelstrom expands violently, growing up to 2 miles in a single day.

**Surge Triggers:**

- Malachar's consciousness stirring in dreams
- Failed containment rituals attempting to seal him
- Large groups approaching the epicenter
- Divine energy from other gods creating resonance
- [[ğŸŒ€ Ascendant Cult]] transformation rituals
- Natural planar alignments and cosmic events

**Surge Warning Signs:**

- Purple lightning increases to constant flashing
- Low hum becomes deafening roar
- Animals flee in all directions
- All corrupted beings turn toward center
- 30 minutes warning before expansion begins

**Survival During Surge:**

- Immediate evacuation recommended
- Reality anchors provide temporary shelter
- [[ğŸš¶ The Pilgrims]] can guide safe routes
- Warded paths may collapse under strain
- Caught inside = severe transformation or death

## **DM Notes**

### **Pacing the Maelstrom:**

- **Early Campaign:** Players explore Outer Ring only, learning about zones
- **Mid Campaign:** Necessity forces Middle Ring ventures with guides
- **Late Campaign:** Inner Ring becomes accessible with divine protection
- **End Game:** Conditions for epicenter entry are discovered/created

### **Running Corruption:**

Track exposure time carefully. Use physical tokens or notes to show transformation progress. Make changes gradual and disturbing - not just mechanical penalties.

### **Malachar's Secret:**

The child is named **Lyra** in her dream. She doesn't know she's a god-prison. The church contains hints: toys that shouldn't exist, impossible paintings, a music box that plays in reversed time. This reveal should be campaign climax material.

### **Solutions:**

- **Containment:** Find/create new tomb using artifacts from [[ğŸ‘ï¸ Isril the Blind]] or [[âš°ï¸ Thyros the Ender]]
- **Divine Intervention:** Awaken another god to restrain Malachar
- **Destruction:** Kill the child/god (dark choice with massive consequences)
- **Transformation Accepted:** Let Malachar wake and try to negotiate with transformed world
