# Marshal Viktor Greyhold

**Race:** Human | **Age:** 52 | **Class:** Fighter 11 (Battle Master)  
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral | **Faction:** Pragmatic Coalition (Military Commander)  
**Location:** Haven's Rest | **CR:** 10

## Appearance

Grizzled veteran with salt-and-pepper beard, scarred face, steel-gray eyes. Wears practical armor (half-plate), no decoration. Missing two fingers on left hand from old battle.

## Personality

**Traits:** Tactical, blunt, pragmatic, protective of troops, distrusts politicians  
**Manner:** Speaks in clipped military terms, assesses threats constantly, never relaxes

## Background

Career soldier from old kingdoms. Rose through ranks on merit. Commands Coalition's 800 guards and military operations. Clashes with Ashford (wants action) and Steelwright (trusts engineer's barriers).

## Stats

**AC:** 19 (half-plate + shield) | **HP:** 110 | **Speed:** 30 ft.  
**STR** 18 (+4) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 16 (+3) | **INT** 12 (+1) | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 10 (+0)

**Abilities:**

- Action Surge, Second Wind, Indomitable (2/day)
- Maneuvers (6): Riposte, Disarming Attack, Commander's Strike, Rally, Tactical Assessment, Menacing Attack
- Extra Attack (2)

**Tactics:** Commands from front, uses terrain, coordinates troops, focuses priority targets

## Relationships

**Allies:** Captain Steelwright (respects), Coalition troops (loyal)  
**Tense:** Chancellor Ashford (political friction)  
**Hostile:** Vigilant Order (sees as fanatics), Ascendant Cult (shoot on sight)

## Plot Hooks

- Hire party for military operation
- Defend Haven's Rest from attack
- Clash with Ashford's policies
- Train refugee militia
