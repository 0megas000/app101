### If She Wakes (Climax Options):

**Option A: Apocalyptic Transformation**
- She's angry, world-ending threat
- Must be defeated or re-imprisoned
- Epic boss fight in shifting reality
- High stakes, heroic finale

**Option B: Alien Benevolence**
- She's not hostile, just incomprehensible
- Must negotiate/communicate
- Understanding her is victory condition
- Philosophical rather than combat resolution

**Option C: Both**
- Combat failed negotiation
- Or negotiation after defeating her champions
- Multi-phase encounter
- Roleplay and rolls both matter

### Moral Complexity:

**She's Not Evil:**
Change isn't inherently good or bad. Evolution requires destruction of old to create new. She represents that truth.

**The Cult Isn't Wrong:**
Their philosophy has merit. World IS changing. Adaptation might be smarter than resistance.

**But Consequences Are Real:**
Forced transformation destroys identity. Millions might die. Not everyone can or should have to change.

**No Right Answer:**
Let players struggle with this. Best campaigns come from hard choices with no perfect solution.
