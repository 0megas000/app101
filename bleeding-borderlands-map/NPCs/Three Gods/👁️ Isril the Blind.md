# Isril the Blind - The All-Seeing God

**Domain:** Prophecy, Knowledge, Sight Beyond Sight
**Status:** Sleeping/Dormant - Most Powerful of the Three
**Alignment:** True Neutral (Cosmic Indifference)
**Power Level:** Greater Deity - Exceeds other sleeping gods

## **Physical Manifestation**
When communed with through oracle vessel, appears as massive stone statue with perfectly blank head and hollow eye sockets. The statue is 15 feet tall, carved from black basalt that seems to absorb light. Despite having no eyes, observers feel intensely watched and judged.

## **Divine Nature & Personality**
### **Core Traits:**
- **Omniscient:** Can see all possible futures simultaneously across all planes
- **Indifferent:** Views mortal concerns as ephemeral and largely irrelevant
- **Pragmatic:** Makes decisions based on cosmic rather than human timescales
- **Unpredictable:** Changes stance based on arguments and offerings presented

### **Communication Style:**
- Speaks only through oracle vessels, never directly
- Answers given as cryptic riddles requiring interpretation  
- Shows glimpses of possible futures rather than clear statements
- Tone varies from cosmic wisdom to petty observations

### **Motivation Factors:**
- **Intellectual curiosity** about how mortals will handle crisis
- **Cosmic balance** - prevents any single outcome from dominating
- **Personal loyalty** to whoever awakens him first (but can be swayed)
- **Entertainment value** - finds mortal struggles amusing or tedious

## **Powers & Abilities**
### **Prophetic Vision:**
- Sees all possible future timelines simultaneously
- Can observe events across multiple planes of existence
- Understands consequences of actions across cosmic timescales
- Perceives probability matrices of different choices

### **Reality Manipulation:**
- Can alter probability to favor or hinder specific outcomes
- Limited ability to change past events through temporal sight
- Can grant prophetic visions to followers and oracles
- Capable of rewriting destiny on local scale

### **Divine Magic:**
- **Divination Domain:** Perfect knowledge of past, present, and potential futures
- **Knowledge Domain:** Instant access to any information that exists
- **Fate Domain:** Limited ability to alter destiny and probability
- **Time Domain:** Perception and minor manipulation of temporal flow

### **Limitations:**
- Cannot act directly without oracle vessel
- Prefers to guide rather than control events
- Cosmic indifference prevents consistent help
- Other gods can potentially counteract his influence

## **Awakening Requirements**
### **Primary Requirement:**
- **The Oracle Vessel:** Willing mortal to serve as his voice and eyes in physical world
- **Current Vessel:** [[Samson the Wanderer]] (unrecognized by most factions)
- **Replacement Process:** Complex ritual requiring surrender of sight and physical strength

### **Communication Method:**
- God cannot truly "awaken" like the others
- Instead, communicates through oracle vessel when properly approached
- Requires ritual recognition of his authority and proper offerings
- Oracle vessel must be present for any divine consultation

### **Consultation Requirements:**
- **Truth Offering:** Knowledge or secrets unknown to others
- **Temporal Alignment:** Specific astronomical configurations
- **Worthy Questions:** Inquiries about cosmic rather than petty concerns
- **Respect Protocol:** Acknowledgment of his supreme authority among the three gods

## **Stance on Planar Convergence**
### **Current Position:**
"The convergence is... inevitable. The question is not whether reality will change, but how mortals will adapt to change. Show me your worthiness to guide this transition."

### **Influencing Factors:**
- **Who awakens him:** First contact creates initial loyalty
- **Quality of offerings:** Better gifts earn more favorable guidance
- **Philosophical arguments:** Compelling reasoning can change his perspective
- **Entertainment value:** Finds interesting solutions more appealing than boring ones

### **Potential Stances:**
- **Support convergence:** If convinced it leads to more interesting reality
- **Oppose convergence:** If persuaded it would create boring static world
- **Guide convergence:** Help mortals navigate change while maintaining cosmic balance
- **Accelerate/delay:** Alter timeline based on arguments presented

## **Relationship with Other Gods**

- **Potential Conflict:** Would oppose if Malachar's changes threaten cosmic balance
- **Cooperation:** Might work together if convinced convergence serves greater purpose

### **Thyros the Ender:**
- **Cosmic Role:** Finality to Isril's infinite possibility, certainty to his knowledge
- **Personal Relationship:** Appreciates Thyros's consistency but finds him limiting
- **Potential Alliance:** Could support if convinced current change must end
- **Fundamental Opposition:** Ending vs. endless possibility creates natural tension

### **Physical Changes:**
- **Blindness:** Eyes become white, clouded, non-functional for normal sight
- **Weakness:** Significant reduction in physical strength and constitution
- **Sensory Enhancement:** Supernatural perception replacing lost abilities

### **Mental Changes:**
- **Prophetic Sight:** Constant visions of possible futures
- **Cosmic Perspective:** Understanding of events across multiple timescales
- **Divine Knowledge:** Access to information beyond normal comprehension
- **Personality Shift:** Gradual adoption of Isril's cosmic indifference

### **Abilities Gained:**
- **Advantage on all Intelligence, Wisdom, and Insight checks**
- **Bonus reaction** each turn from foresight
- **Oracle's Sight** (Action): All allies gain advantage on attacks/saves for 1 round
- **Future Knowledge:** Learn exact next intended action of all visible NPCs
- **Prophetic Warnings:** Provide cryptic guidance about upcoming dangers

### **The Price:**
- Permanent blindness and physical weakness
- Gradual loss of human emotional connections
- Increasing difficulty relating to normal mortal concerns
- Risk of madness from overwhelming cosmic awareness

## **Secrets & Hidden Knowledge**
### **True Nature of Crisis:**
Isril knows the planar convergence is part of natural cosmic cycle occurring every few millennia. The "crisis" is actually reality evolving to next stage, with mortal adaptation determining the outcome.

### **God Relationships:**
The three gods were never truly separate entities but aspects of cosmic forces that became individualized during last convergence. They can potentially merge or separate again based on mortal choices.

### **Ultimate Timeline:**
Without intervention, convergence completes in approximately 8 months, fundamentally altering reality. Mortal actions can accelerate, delay, or redirect but not prevent cosmic change.

### **Hidden Purpose:**
Isril's true interest is in whether mortals will evolve wisdom to match their power, or destroy themselves through fear and conflict. The crisis is partially a test of species maturity.
