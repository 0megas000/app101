# Dr. Elara Voss

## **Quick Reference**
- **Age:** 43
- **Race:** Human
- **Class:** Wizard (School of Transmutation) Level 12
- **Threat Level:** Medium (Intellectual authority, not combat focused)
- **Reputation:** Brilliant but obsessive researcher

## **Physical Description**
A woman in her early forties with prematurely gray hair from years of stress and late-night research. Sharp green eyes that seem to analyze everything with scientific intensity. Wears practical academic robes with numerous pockets containing research instruments, measuring devices, and sample containers. Dark circles under her eyes suggest chronic sleep deprivation from obsessive work habits.

**Distinctive Features:**
- Always carries a leather journal filled with notes and calculations
- Hands stained with various alchemical reagents
- Tends to gesture emphatically when discussing theories
- Unconsciously adjusts wire-rimmed spectacles when thinking

## **Personality Profile**
### **Core Traits:**
- **Brilliant:** Genuine genius-level intellect with innovative problem-solving
- **Obsessive:** Cannot let go of research questions or theoretical problems
- **Methodical:** Approaches every problem with systematic scientific methodology
- **Passionate:** Becomes animated and almost manic when discussing research

### **Ideals:**
- Knowledge and empirical evidence can solve any problem, including divine mysteries
- Scientific method is superior to faith-based or traditional approaches
- Education and rational thinking will ultimately triumph over superstition
- Progress requires challenging established beliefs and authorities

### **Bonds:**
- **Thornwick Academy:** Sees university as bastion of rationality in chaotic world
- **Scientific Community:** Maintains connections with scholars across multiple kingdoms
- **Her Research:** Personally invested in proving corruption has scientific causes
- **Student Welfare:** Genuinely cares about young researchers under her guidance

### **Flaws:**
- **Anti-Religious Obsession:** Personal vendetta against faith-based explanations
- **Workaholic Tendencies:** Neglects personal health and relationships for research
- **Intellectual Arrogance:** Dismissive of others' concerns if not scientifically grounded
- **Ethical Blindness:** Sometimes ignores moral implications of dangerous experiments

## **Background & History**
### **Academic Career:**
Former Chair of Planar Studies at the prestigious Royal University of Asterhaven. Lost her position after publishing papers that challenged religious doctrine with scientific evidence about divine phenomena. The academic scandal destroyed her career at traditional institutions.

### **Personal Tragedy:**
Lost her younger brother and his family in the early corruption outbreak while she was away at a conference. Guilt over her absence and horror at their fate drives her obsessive need to understand and control the crisis through science.

### **Current Position:**
Recruited by [[üéì Thornwick Academy]] as crisis began, given unprecedented resources and authority to lead scientific response. Now commands respect from displaced academics region-wide while pursuing personal mission to prove gods are merely powerful extraplanar entities.

## **Current Goals & Motivations**
### **Professional Objectives:**
- Develop technological solutions to reverse corruption effects
- Establish scientific authority over religious explanations of crisis
- Create comprehensive theoretical framework explaining planar convergence
- Train new generation of researchers to continue rational approach

### **Personal Mission:**
- Prove the sleeping gods are powerful but non-divine extraplanar entities
- Find evidence that religious factions caused or worsened the crisis
- Vindicate her career-destroying theories about divine phenomena
- Prevent others from suffering losses like her family experienced

### **Secret Goals:**
- Locate and study the sleeping gods directly using scientific methods
- Develop technology capable of controlling or binding divine-level entities
- Establish Academy as supreme authority replacing religious institutions
- Find way to bring her brother's family back using dimensional science
