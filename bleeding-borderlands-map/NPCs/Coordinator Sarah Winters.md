# Coordinator Sarah Winters

**Race:** Human | **Age:** 29 | **Class:** Rogue 7 (Mastermind)  
**Alignment:** Neutral Good | **Faction:** Pragmatic Coalition (Logistics)  
**Location:** Haven's Rest | **CR:** 5

## Appearance

Efficient-looking woman with dark hair in tight bun, practical clothing, constantly writing in ledgers. Wears reading spectacles, carries multiple notebooks, ink stains on fingers.

## Personality

**Traits:** Organized, efficient, detail-oriented, caring but pragmatic, never forgets a number  
**Manner:** Speaks quickly, multitasks constantly, frustrated by inefficiency, surprisingly warm with refugees

## Background

Former merchant's daughter who applied trade logistics to humanitarian crisis. Coordinates supply distribution to 45,000+ refugees. Tracks every sack of grain, every blanket, every refugee location. Exhausted but refuses to stop.

## Stats

**AC:** 15 (leather + DEX) | **HP:** 42 | **Speed:** 30 ft.  
**STR** 10 (+0) | **DEX** 16 (+3) | **CON** 12 (+1) | **INT** 18 (+4) | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 14 (+2)

**Abilities:**

- Master of Intrigue (mimic speech patterns, read people)
- Sneak Attack (4d6)
- Expertise: Investigation, Insight, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand

**Special:** Knows exact location/needs of every refugee camp, supply cache, distribution route. Living database.

## Relationships

**Works With:** Chancellor Ashford (boss), Captain Steelwright (coordinates construction), Marshal Greyhold (supplies troops)  
**Contacts:** Has informant in every major camp, knows most refugees by name

## Plot Hooks

- Commission supply run to remote camp
- Investigate supply theft
- Expose corruption in distribution
- Protect her from assassins (knows too much)
- Help optimize failing supply route
