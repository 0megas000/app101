## Appearance
Appears as unremarkable homeless man with clouded white eyes. Tattered robes, worn walking stick, carries small bundle of possessions. Usually camps in same three spots around [[ğŸ“ Thornwick Academy]]'s [[The Outskirts|outskirt]]. Most people walk past without noticing.

## Personality

**Seemingly Rambling:** Speaks in riddles, non-sequiturs, and seemingly random observations. Actually answering questions people haven't asked yet or referring to events from different timelines.

**Deliberately Unassuming:** Could reveal himself as powerful oracle. Chooses not to. Tests those who seek knowledge by making them work for it. Wisdom requires effort.

**Genuinely Kind:** Beneath mystery, truly cares about people. Warns of dangers, guides lost travelers, shares food despite having little. Never asks for anything in return.

**Unknowable Agenda:** Serves [[ğŸ‘ï¸ Isril the Blind]] but what does Isril want? Samson knows but won't say directly. Players must figure out if helping him helps or harms them.
