## The Barriers (His Life's Work)

**Design Principles:**
- Modular sections (can build anywhere)
- Local materials (no complex supply chains)
- Unskilled labor (refugees can help build)
- Quick assembly (2 days per section)
- 60% effectiveness (slows, doesn't stop corruption)

**Current Projects:**
- 12 barriers around corruption zones
- 3 completed, 5 under construction, 4 planned
- Each requires constant maintenance
- Never enough resources or people

**The Problem:**
- Corruption spreads faster than he can build
- Each barrier buys time but not solution
- He knows this but builds anyway
- Because what else can he do?
