# Wave-Walker Torin

**Race:** Human (Pilgrim) | **Age:** Unknown (appears 40s) | **Class:** Ranger 9 (Horizon Walker)  
**Alignment:** True Neutral | **Faction:** The Pilgrims (Guide)  
**Location:** Silverflow River, traveling | **CR:** 7

## Appearance

Weather-worn riverman with sun-darkened skin, calloused hands. Wears layered waterproof clothing. Eyes have seen too much reality distortion. Moves with unnatural ease between solid/water.

## Personality

**Traits:** Silent, observant, charges fair rates, never abandons clients, speaks in metaphors  
**Manner:** More comfortable on water than land. Commune with river. Knows every current, eddy, corruption pocket.

## Background

Riverman who adapted to corruption by accepting it without embracing it. Can navigate normal and distorted waterways. One of few who can guide safely through corrupted sections of Silverflow.

## Stats

**AC:** 16 (studded leather + DEX) | **HP:** 72 | **Speed:** 30 ft., swim 40 ft.  
**STR** 14 | **DEX** 18 (+4) | **CON** 14 | **INT** 12 | **WIS** 16 (+3) | **CHA** 10

**Abilities:**

- Planar Warrior (3d8 force damage)
- Detect Portal (sense reality tears within 1 mile)
- Ethereal Step (bonus action, become ethereal for 1 turn)

**Special:** Corruption-adapted - automatically succeeds on first corruption save per day

**Guide Services:** 50 GP per person per day (river travel), 200 GP through corrupted waters

## Tactics

Avoids combat when possible. If forced, uses Ethereal Step to escape/reposition. Protects clients first. Knows emergency exits from any river position.

## Relationships

**Respected By:** All factions (neutral, reliable)  
**Network:** Knows every Pilgrim, riverman, smuggler on Silverflow

## Plot Hooks

- Hire him for river expedition
- He knows secret river routes
- Rescue him from river pirates
- Commission him for smuggling run
- Learn Pilgrim adaptation techniques
