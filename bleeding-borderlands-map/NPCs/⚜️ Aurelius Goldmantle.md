## Appearance

Always dressed in golden ceremonial armor that gleams even in dim light‚Äîsymbols of the [[Vigilant Order]] etched into every surface. White cloak trimmed with gold thread flows behind him. ## Personality Traits

**Zealous and Uncompromising**  
Once moderate religious leader, now extremist driven by personal tragedy. No tolerance for deviation from Order doctrine. Sees compromise as weakness and weakness as sin that kills.

**Grief-Driven**  
Lost wife Elise and children Thomas (12) and Catherine (8) to early corruption outbreak. Blames himself for not being faithful enough to prevent it. Channeled grief into religious fanaticism.

**Genuinely Believes**  
Not cynical power-seeker. Truly convinced [[Vigilant Order]] represents only path to salvation. Every atrocity committed in pursuit of what he sees as greater good.

**Charismatic Leader**  
Inspires fierce loyalty in followers. Powerful orator who can move crowds to tears or fury. Genuinely cares for those who follow Order‚Äîjust not for anyone else.

**Increasingly Unstable**  
Grief and pressure pushing him toward dangerous decisions. Starting to see heresy everywhere. Trust eroding even among allies. Becoming the tyrant he once would have opposed.

**Military Brilliance**  
Former banking guild leader turned religious warrior. Combines financial acumen with strategic thinking. Made [[Vigilant Order]] most powerful faction through wealth and organization.

## Background & History

### Before the Crisis (Ages 30-57)

**Banking Dynasty:**
- Inherited Goldmantle Banking Guild at age 30
- Expanded to become wealthiest institution in region
- Known for fair dealing and charitable works
- Moderate religious believer, attended services regularly
- Respected in business and social circles

**Happy Family Life:**
- Married Elise Brightwater at age 35 (political alliance that became love)
- Son Thomas born when he was 40
- Daughter Catherine born when he was 44
- Country estate outside [[Goldspire]]
- Balanced work and family successfully

### The Tragedy (Age 57)

**The Corruption Outbreak:**
- Early corruption struck country estate without warning
- Aurelius in [[Goldspire]] on banking business
- Received news estate consumed by reality distortion
- Rode through night to reach family
- Found estate in dimensional flux

**What He Found:**
- Wife Elise transformed into something unrecognizable
- Tried to approach, reality rejected him (burn scars)
- Children's bodies twisted by corruption
- Spent three days trying to recover remains
- Finally forced to flee as corruption spread
- Bodies left behind, consumed entirely

**The Breakdown:**
- Locked himself in [[The Golden Citadel]] for three months
- Refused food, barely drank water
- Priests heard him screaming, arguing with himself
- Emerged changed fundamentally
- Hair white-streaked, eyes cold, faith absolute

### Religious Conversion (Ages 57-60)

**The Revelation:**
- Believed family died because his faith was insufficient
- If he'd been truly devout, gods would have protected them
- Saw their deaths as punishment for his lukewarm faith
- Vowed never to be "insufficient" again

**Radical Actions:**
- Donated entire personal fortune to [[Vigilant Order]] (billions)
- Renounced banking guild leadership
- Took vows of service to Order
- Rose through ranks through zeal and organizational ability
- Became High Inquisitor within three years (unprecedented)

**Building Power:**
- Transformed Order from spiritual organization to militant faction
- Established Inquisition to root out heresy
- Created templar army 800 strong
- Built [[The Golden Citadel]] into fortress-cathedral
- Made [[Goldspire]] center of religious power

### Current Era (Ages 60-62)

**Supreme Authority:**
- Absolute control of [[Vigilant Order]]
- Wealth and military might
- Political influence across region
- Feared and respected in equal measure

**Growing Extremism:**
- Seeing heresy in smaller infractions
- Trust eroding (suspects even loyal followers)
- [[Sister-Marshall Vex Ironhold]] concerned about his stability
- Hallucinations of dead wife guiding him
- Approaching breaking point

## Goals & Motivations

**Primary Goal:** Purify the region through absolute adherence to [[Vigilant Order]] doctrine. Eliminate all heresy, establish religious authority over all.

**Secondary Goals:**
- Atone for family's death through perfect devotion
- Destroy [[Empirical Academy]] (greatest heresy)
- Eliminate [[Ascendant Cult]] (ultimate abomination)
- Convert or exile [[The Pilgrims]]
- Prove gods will save the truly faithful

**Obsession:** Finding and executing those who "spread corruption through lack of faith"‚Äîwhich increasingly means anyone who disagrees with him.

**Secret Goal:** Resurrect his family through perfect faith (impossible, but he believes)

**Fears:**
- Family's death was meaningless
- His faith might be insufficient even now
- Corruption approaching [[Goldspire]] despite his devotion
- Losing control of Order to moderates
- Discovering he's wrong about everything
- Dying without atonement
