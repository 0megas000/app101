# Librarian Cornelius Dustbottom

**Title:** Assistant Librarian & Restricted Archives Keeper | **Age:** 27 **Affiliation:** [[üéì Thornwick Academy]] / [[üìö The Great Library]] **Role:** Gatekeeper of Forbidden Knowledge **Status:** Nervous, Conflicted, Secretly Compromised

## **Appearance**

**Physical:**

- Thin and pale from spending life indoors
- Height: 5'8", slight build, poor posture from hunching over books
- Messy brown hair that he's always pushing out of his eyes
- Round wire-frame glasses (constantly adjusting them)
- Dark circles under eyes from poor sleep
- Ink-stained fingers permanently
- Looks younger than his age (baby-faced)

**Clothing:**

- Worn librarian's robes (gray with Academy insignia)
- Comfortable but shabby - can't afford better
- Always has dust on shoulders and knees
- Pockets stuffed with bookmarks, notes, quills
- Carries a large ring of keys (his badge of authority)
- Wears father's old pocket watch (doesn't work)

**Demeanor:**

- Jumpy, startles easily
- Fidgets constantly (adjusts glasses, touches keys)
- Avoids eye contact when lying
- Speaks quickly when nervous (which is often)
- Hunches defensively around authority figures
- Relaxes only when discussing books

## **Personality**

### **Core Traits:**

- **Protective:** Genuinely believes dangerous knowledge should be restricted
- **Anxious:** Constant worry about everything
- **Conflicted:** Doing something he knows is wrong out of desperation
- **Intelligent:** Brilliant at cataloging and understanding texts
- **Lonely:** Isolated by job and secrets
- **Dutiful:** Trying to honor father's legacy despite circumstances

### **Strengths:**

- Encyclopedic knowledge of library contents
- Perfect memory for book locations
- Can assess text danger levels accurately
- Skilled at forgery and copying (unfortunately)
- Genuinely wants to protect people from harmful knowledge
- Dedicated to proper research protocols

### **Weaknesses:**

- Terrible liar (obvious tells when nervous)
- Easily intimidated by authority
- Paralyzed by guilt over secret activities
- Financially desperate and exploitable
- Can't say no when pressured
- Fear of [[üß™ Dr. Elara Voss]] clouds judgment

## **Background**

### **Family Legacy:**

**Father: Master Librarian Aldous Dustbottom**

- Held position of Head Restricted Archives for 30 years
- Renowned scholar of dangerous and forbidden texts
- Understood better than anyone what knowledge could destroy
- Loving but distant father (married to his work)
- Only family Cornelius had (mother died in childbirth)

**The Disappearance:**

- 18 months ago, volunteered for [[üß™ Empirical Academy]] expedition
- Voss needed expert on pre-convergence binding rituals
- Aldous went to [[üå™Ô∏è The Maelstrom]] Middle Ring
- Expedition was ambushed, scattered
- Aldous never returned
- Body never found - presumed dead

**The Inheritance:**

- Cornelius promoted to Assistant Librarian at age 25
- Given keys to Restricted Archives (provisional)
- Expected to know father's systems (doesn't fully)
- **Father's Master Key Missing:** Would unlock deepest archives
- Suspected his father had it when he disappeared
- Without it, some sections remain sealed even to Cornelius

### **Growing Up in the Library:**

- Raised among books after mother's death
- Father taught him cataloging, preservation, security
- Learned to love and respect knowledge
- Also learned which books could break minds
- Understood responsibility of being gatekeeper
- Never wanted this burden but accepts it as duty

### **Current Situation:**

**The Debt:**

- Father left significant debts (unknown to Cornelius until after death)
- Owed to **Merchant-Lord [[Merchant-Lord Cassius Vale]]** for "research materials"
- 500 gold pieces - fortune for a librarian
- Vale's collectors visit monthly, "understanding" but firm
- Debt or get removed from position (Vale has influence)

**The Compromise:** Six months ago, Vale made an offer:

- "Simply provide copies of certain texts"
- "Nothing too dangerous, just... valuable information"
- "Debt will be forgiven over time"
- Cornelius knew it was wrong but saw no other option

**The Operation:**

- Cornelius copies restricted texts after hours
- Uses special paper that doesn't trigger wards
- Delivers copies to Vale's agents in [[üõí Scholar's Market]]
- So far: 23 texts copied and sold
- Hasn't noticed that some buyers are [[üåÄ Ascendant Cult]] agents
- Doesn't know some texts are reaching [[üëÅÔ∏è Divine Awakeners]]
- Guilt eating him alive

## **Role at The Great Library**

### **Official Duties:**

**Restricted Archives Management:**

- Control access to dangerous texts (levels 4-7 restriction)
- Evaluate research requests for approval
- Monitor scholars reading restricted materials
- Maintain security wards and locks
- Report suspicious research interests
- Catalog new acquisitions

**General Library Support:**

- Assist Head Librarian with administration
- Train new library assistants
- Lead tours for important visitors
- Emergency response for book-related incidents
- Liaison with [[üß™ Empirical Academy]] research needs

### **The Restricted Archives:**

**What's Stored There:**

- Pre-convergence binding rituals
- Texts on communicating with sleeping gods
- Reality manipulation theories
- Dangerous magical research
- Corrupted or corruption-causing texts
- Books that drove previous readers insane
- Knowledge that could destabilize reality further

**Security Levels:**

- **Level 4:** Requires faculty approval
- **Level 5:** Requires department head approval
- **Level 6:** Requires [[Dean Aldric Thornwick]] approval
- **Level 7:** Requires Cornelius + Dean + armed guard present
- **Level 8 (Sealed):** Requires father's master key (missing)

**What Level 8 Contains:** Cornelius doesn't fully know - father never told him everything. Suspects:

- Direct god-summoning rituals
- Reality-ending knowledge
- Pre-god civilization texts
- Original Maelstrom cause information
- Father's personal research notes

## **The Secret Operation**

### **How It Works:**

**Step 1 - The Request:** Vale's agent approaches with "shopping list"

- Usually 2-3 texts per month
- Titles are specific, someone knows what they want
- Cornelius receives list at dead drop in market

**Step 2 - The Copy:**

- Works late night when library empty
- Uses specialized paper (Vale provides) that bypasses wards
- Perfect transcription (his one actual skill serving evil)
- Takes 4-8 hours per text
- Stress is destroying him

**Step 3 - The Delivery:**

- Hides copies in hollowed book covers
- Leaves at market stall (pottery vendor, actually Vale's agent)
- Receives payment (goes straight to debt)
- Never sees actual buyers
- Pretends not to know where it goes

**What He's Copied So Far:**

- Texts on planar convergence mechanics
- Pre-kingdom god-binding techniques
- Reality anchor construction methods
- Corruption adaptation theories
- Communication rituals for sleeping deities
- Transformation acceleration processes
- Several others he tries not to remember

**The Buyers (Unknown to Him):**

- **[[Merchant-Lord Cassius Vale]]:** Resells to highest bidders
- **[[üåÄ Ascendant Cult]]:** Using transformation texts
- **[[üëÅÔ∏è Divine Awakeners]]:** Using communion rituals
- **[[‚öúÔ∏è Vigilant Order]]:** Ironically, buying some to prevent others from having them
- **Foreign Powers:** Interested parties from other kingdoms
- **Private Collectors:** Wealthy individuals wanting forbidden knowledge

**The Guilt:**

- Barely sleeps from nightmares
- Knows he's betraying father's legacy
- Understands people might die from this knowledge
- But debt and fear of losing position paralyze him
- Wants to confess but terrified of consequences
- Prays someone will stop him so he doesn't have to choose

## **Relationship with Dr. Voss**

### **Pure Terror:**

**Why He Fears Her:**

- Voss's reputation for ruthlessness
- Her anti-religious crusade makes her unpredictable
- She's eliminated rivals and obstacles before
- Has power to fire him or worse
- Suspects she'd consider his copying "useful" or "treason" depending on her mood
- Her eyes seem to see through people

**Interactions:**

- Cornelius stammers and sweats around her
- Rushes to fulfill her research requests
- Can't say no to anything she demands
- Lives in terror she'll discover his secret
- Suspects she knows something but is waiting

**The Complication:** Voss has been requesting many restricted texts lately:

- Some overlap with what Vale wants
- Makes Cornelius wonder if Voss is somehow connected
- Or if multiple people want same dangerous knowledge
- Terrified of being caught in crossfire

**Her View of Him:**

- "Useful but weak"
- "Like his father but without the spine"
- Sees him as temporary placeholder until better option appears
- Doesn't suspect the copying (yet)
- Would be furious if she found out

## **Relationship with Players**

### **As Gatekeeper:**

**Initial Interaction:**

- Nervous, rule-bound, says no to everything at first
- Cites regulations obsessively
- Needs "proper authorization in triplicate"
- Actually stalling while he decides if request is dangerous

**Earning Access:**

- Proper channels (dean approval) = automatic access
- Convincing him research is important and safe
- Appealing to his better nature
- Proving you respect dangerous knowledge
- NOT threatening or intimidating (makes him shut down)

**What He Can Provide:**

- Access to restricted texts (with approval)
- Knowledge about library contents
- Historical research assistance
- Warnings about which books are genuinely dangerous
- Book recommendations for safe alternatives
- Translation help with ancient languages

### **The Confession Opportunity:**

If players are kind, understanding, non-judgmental:

- Cornelius might break down and confess
- Desperately wants someone to help him escape this
- Needs allies to protect him from Vale
- Could provide information about buyers
- Might help investigate what's happening to copied texts

**If Players Threaten or Judge Harshly:**

- Shuts down completely
- Flees or reports them
- Continues operation in increased fear
- Becomes long-term problem

## **Daily Routine**

**Morning (7 AM - 12 PM):**

- Open library, check for break-ins
- Morning anxiety about being discovered
- Process access requests
- Assist researchers with safe texts
- Avoid Voss if possible

**Afternoon (12 PM - 6 PM):**

- Cataloging and preservation work
- Supervise restricted reading rooms
- Administrative paperwork
- Check dead drop for new "requests" (with dread)
- Try to eat lunch (usually can't)

**Evening (6 PM - 10 PM):**

- Close library to public
- Final checks and security
- If no copying scheduled: goes home
- If copying scheduled: waits for building to empty

**Night (Copying Nights):**

- 10 PM - 2 AM: Transcription work
- Hands shaking, jumping at every sound
- Crying quietly while working
- Praying nobody discovers him
- Hating himself with every word copied

**At Home:**

- Tiny apartment near library
- Can't afford better on salary
- Surrounded by books (comfort and curse)
- Stares at father's broken pocket watch
- Dreams of finding father and being free

**The Hope:** If father is alive and found:

- Has the master key to deepest archives

- Would know how to fix everything
- Could protect Cornelius from consequences
- Might contain knowledge critical to stopping Maelstrom
