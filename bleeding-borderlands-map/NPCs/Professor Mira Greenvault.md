## **Secrets & Complications**

### **Hidden Guilt:**

Marcus told her about "weird readings" at his mining site days before the Maelstrom. She was busy with tenure review, told him it was probably nothing. Believes if she'd investigated, she could have warned people, saved him.

### **The Sample:**

Found a unique stone sample near where Marcus disappeared. It shows properties that shouldn't exist‚Äîboth corrupted and uncorrupted simultaneously. Hasn't told Voss because she's secretly trying to understand it herself. Hopes it's connected to Marcus somehow.

### **Voss's Real Plan:**

Suspects Voss's anti-religious agenda is becoming dangerous obsession. Worries expeditions are more about proving gods wrong than keeping people safe. Doesn't know what to do with this concern.

### **Burnout:**

Approaching mental/physical collapse from overwork and grief. Hiding exhaustion from everyone. One more major crisis might break her.
