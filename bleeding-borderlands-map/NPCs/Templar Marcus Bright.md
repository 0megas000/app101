# Templar Marcus Bright

**Race:** Human | **Age:** 34 | **Class:** Paladin 8 (Oath of Vengeance)  
**Alignment:** Lawful Evil | **Faction:** Vigilant Order (Templar)  
**Location:** Goldspire (Templar Barracks) | **CR:** 6

## Appearance

Handsome, clean-shaven, golden armor gleaming. Everything a paladin should look like. Cold eyes betray zealotry beneath noble exterior.

## Personality

**Traits:** Zealous, ruthless, charismatic to believers, cruel to "heretics"  
**Darkness:** Enjoys punishment of heretics. Believes torture is holy duty. Volunteers for executions.

## Background

Joined Order young, rose fast through devotion and results. Leads raid teams against cult cells. High body count. Respected by superiors, feared by subordinates. Genuinely believes cruelty serves divine purpose.

## Stats

**AC:** 20 (plate + shield) | **HP:** 68 | **Speed:** 30 ft.  
**STR** 18 | **DEX** 10 | **CON** 14 | **INT** 10 | **WIS** 12 | **CHA** 16

**Abilities:**

- Divine Smite (4d8)
- Vow of Enmity (advantage vs. one enemy)
- Relentless Avenger (pursue fleeing enemies)
- Aura of Protection (+3 to all saves within 10 ft.)

**Spells:** DC 14, +6 to hit. Compelled Duel, Thunderous Smite, Branding Smite, Hold Person, Lesser Restoration

## Tactics

Targets corrupted/suspected heretics first. Uses Vow of Enmity on "greatest threat to faith". Divine Smites without mercy. Pursues fleeing enemies relentlessly.

## Relationships

**Respected By:** Aurelius (valuable asset), Vex (effective soldier)  
**Leads:** Strike team of 10 templars (CR 3)

## Plot Hooks

- Pursues party as suspected heretics
- Leads raid on innocent targets
- Forces moral choice (stop him = enemy of Order)
- Falls from grace (corruption or doubt)
- Party must work with him despite hatred
