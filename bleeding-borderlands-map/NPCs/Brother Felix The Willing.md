# Brother Felix "The Willing"

**Race:** Human (Corrupted) | **Age:** 26 | **Class:** Cleric 7 (Malachar Domain)  
**Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral | **Faction:** Ascendant Cult (Recruiter)  
**Location:** Crossroads End (The Speaking Circle) | **CR:** 4

## Appearance

Young man with gentle eyes, warm smile. Early corruption: left arm translucent (phases slightly), hair shifts color slowly. Wears simple robes, welcoming demeanor. Not frightening - that's the point.

## Personality

**Traits:** Genuinely kind, believes transformation is gift, patient with skeptics, never forces  
**Approach:** Offers acceptance, not threats. Speaks of liberation, not destruction. Shows own transformation as beautiful, not horrifying.

## Background

Orphan who found family in cult. Voluntarily transformed. Truly happy with change. Perfect recruiter because he's sincere - he genuinely believes he's helping people find belonging.

## Stats

**AC:** 14 (natural armor) | **HP:** 52 | **Speed:** 30 ft.  
**STR** 10 | **DEX** 12 | **CON** 14 | **INT** 12 | **WIS** 18 (+4) | **CHA** 16 (+3)

**Spells (Cleric 7):** DC 15, +7 to hit. Healing Word, Bless, Cure Wounds, Hold Person, Spiritual Weapon, Spirit Guardians

**Phase Touch:** 1/day, touch becomes incorporeal, pass through object/creature

## Tactics

Never attacks first. Uses Healing Word on wounded (even enemies). Demonstrates corruption's benefits, not threats. If attacked, defends but tries to flee, not kill.

## Relationships

**Loyal To:** Seraphina (moderate faction), opposes Kaine's extremism  
**Approach:** Offers party transformation as "gift", respects refusal

## Plot Hooks

- Recruit vulnerable NPC
- Debate philosophy with party
- Help him despite being enemy
- Use him as inside source
- Rescue him from Kaine's purge
