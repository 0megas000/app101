- Born to merchant family in pre-convergence trade hub
- Legitimate business until parents killed in trade dispute
- 17-year-old Tobias inherited nothing but debts
- Learned survival on streets, started "acquiring" goods
- Natural talent for finding what people needed
- Built reputation as reliable fence and dealer

### **Rise in Underworld (Ages 30-50):**

- Joined [[üïµÔ∏è Helping Hand]] syndicate at 32

- Never violent unless absolutely necessary (preferred broker)

- Survived three gang wars through careful neutrality
- Became too valuable to eliminate (even to enemies)

### **The Maelstrom Era (Last 3 Years):**

- Crisis created unprecedented opportunity
- Desperate people need supplies, weapons, information
- Multiple factions willing to pay premium prices
- Refugees arrive with valuables to trade
- Chaos provides perfect cover for operations
- Business never better (morally questionable but true)

### **Current Situation:**

- Operating in [[üéì Thornwick Academy]] area primarily
- Moves between university district and refugee camps
- Serves students, faculty, refugees, faction agents
- Protected by bribes to city guard and Order templars
- [[üïµÔ∏è Helping Hand]] uses him to monitor all factions

## **Operations**

### **What He Sells:**

**Legal (But Expensive):**

- Expedition supplies (better quality than official)
- Rare books and research materials
- Alchemical components
- Quality weapons and armor
- Magical trinkets and minor enchantments
- Hard-to-find specialty goods

**Gray Market (Questionable):**

- Restricted materials (not illegal but controlled)
- Forged documents and credentials
- "Acquired" equipment from various sources

- Access to restricted areas (bribes included)

**Illegal (Absolutely):**

**Information (Most Valuable):**

- NPC whereabouts and secrets
- Safe routes through dangerous areas
- Who's buying what (intelligence goldmine)
- Rumors, gossip, and verified facts
