## **Divine Blessing: The Far-Sight**

### **What She Sees:**

**True Sight:**

- Reads auras and emotions like text

**Mental:**

- Prophecies are symbolic, not clear
- Vow prevents direct action
- Information she shares is limited by divine will
- Some futures are uncertain even to her
