## Quick Reference

**Role:** Morally complex antagonist, crime boss with heart, anti-villain  
**Alignment:** Neutral Evil (believes Neutral Good)  
**Voice:** Warm, maternal, hides steel core  
**Appearance:** Grandmotherly woman who runs ruthless criminal empire

## Personality

**Maternal Criminal:** Genuinely cares for "her people" (syndicate members, refugees under protection). Just happens to care through criminal means.

**Started Noble:** Began as legitimate refugee aid. System failed people. Crime worked better. Slippery slope to current state.

**Ruthless When Necessary:** Warm and kind 90% of time. Other 10%, has people killed efficiently. Compartmentalizes this easily.

**Community Leader:** Refugees see her as protector. Authorities see her as problem. Both right.

## Operations

**Black Market Food:** Steals Coalition supplies, sells below market price to refugees  
**Protection Rackets:** "Insurance" against bandits/disasters (causes some herself)  
**Smuggling Network:** Moves people/goods through corruption zones  
**Information Brokering:** Sells intelligence to all factions  
**Recruitment:** Employs desperate refugees, gives purpose and income

## Territory

Controls sections of [[Crossroads End]]'s [[The Disputed District]] and refugee camps. Also operates in [[Ironhold]] camps. Has agents in most cities.

## Relationships

**[[Mayor Elspeth Copperton]]:** Bribes her, controls half city council  
**[[Captain Jana Ironfeld]]:** Town guard too small to stop her  
**[[Chancellor Mira Ashford]]:** Coalition sees her as problem they can't solve  
**Refugees:** See her as hero who provides where authorities fail

## Moral Complexity

**Good Actions:**
- Feeds people authorities abandon
- Protects refugees from bandits
- Employs those no one else will
- Cheaper services than legitimate options

**Bad Actions:**
- Steals from limited supplies
- Violent toward rivals
- Corrupts officials
- Normalizes crime as solution

## Quest Hooks

**Against Her:**
- Coalition wants syndicate stopped
- Investigate theft from supply warehouses
- Protect official from assassination
- Rescue person from forced recruitment

**With Her:**
- She offers better pay than authorities
- Protect refugee community from bandits
- Smuggle supplies through dangerous routes
- Information about other factions

**Moral Gray:**
- She's only one feeding certain refugees
- Stopping her means people starve
- But allowing her means corruption spreads
- No good answer
